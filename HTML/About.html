Founder

Mr.Louis Brandt

Louis Brandt and his brother Cesar began their careers as Louis Brandt & Fils after acquiring both their father's business and his passion for watchmaking after his death in 1879. They soon began selling expert quality watches such as the 'Labrador' that were considered by many to be far beyond their time. Brandt's dedication to the industry led to the winning of multiple awards and in 1969, Omega watches were worn by members of NASA and thus became the first watches on the moon.
